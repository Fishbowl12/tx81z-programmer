EG Bias Controls

EG bias affects the range of the EGs as you blow harder on the breath controller.

BC

This is the overall affect of the breath controller on the EG bias. This can alternatively affect the aftertouch. See the AT -> BC setting in the section on system setup.

Sensitivity (EBS)

These sliders determine how much the EG bias affects each operator. Operator 1 is on the left, followed by 2, 3 and 4.
